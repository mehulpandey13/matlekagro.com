CULTIVATOR


The cultivator, an indispensable tool for every farmer, serves as the backbone of agricultural efficiency. Whether tilling the soil for planting, controlling weeds during growth, or aerating the land post-harvest, this versatile implement ensures optimal soil health and crop yield. We equip your farm with our reliable cultivators and set the foundation for a bountiful harvest.

SPRAYER


Sprayers are a cornerstone of modern agriculture, facilitating the precise application of essential treatments. From backpack sprayers for small-scale operations to tow-behind models for large fields, our range is designed to ensure accuracy, efficiency, and ease of use. With durable construction, innovative technology, and superior performance, our sprayers deliver consistent results, helping farmers optimize crop health and yield.

ELECTRIC PUMPS


Electric pumps are the lifeline of modern irrigation, providing a reliable source of water for agricultural operations. Whether you need to draw water from a well, a pond, or a river, our pumps are designed to deliver consistent performance under a range of conditions. With user-friendly controls, low maintenance requirements, and energy-saving features, our electric pumps are an indispensable asset for any farming operation.

MILLS


Mills are the heart of the farm, grinding grains into flour or meal for human consumption and animal feed. From hand-operated models for small-scale production to large, electric-powered mills for commercial operations, our range is built to last and deliver reliable performance day after day. With our mills, you can process your own grains, ensuring quality and freshness, and tailor feed mixes to your specific requirements.

CUTTER MACHINE


The cutter machine is the backbone of modern agriculture, facilitating the precise harvest and processing of crops. From hand-held models for small-scale operations to motorized variants for large fields, our range is designed to ensure accuracy, efficiency, and ease of use. With durable construction, innovative technology, and superior performance, our cutter machines deliver consistent results, helping farmers optimize crop health and yield.

BASIC EQUIPMENT


Basic equipment is the backbone of modern agriculture, facilitating precise and efficient work on the farm.
